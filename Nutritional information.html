Nutritional Information

Fruits

  • Apple: 1 medium-sized apple contains about 95 calories, 25 grams of carbohydrates, 4 grams of fiber, and provides vitamin C and potassium.
  • Banana: 1 medium-sized banana contains about 105 calories, 27 grams of carbohydrates, 3 grams of fiber, and provides vitamin B6 and potassium.

Vegetables

  • Spinach: 1 cup of cooked spinach contains about 41 calories, 7 grams of carbohydrates, 4 grams of fiber, and provides vitamin A, vitamin K, and iron.
  • Broccoli: 1 cup of chopped broccoli contains about 55 calories, 11 grams of carbohydrates, 5 grams of fiber, and provides vitamin C, vitamin K, and folate.
